Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 September 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how "Lek" by Blank Canvas Designs Private Limited handles and protects your personal data. By using the app, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described below.

1. General Information

"Lek" is an iOS application that allows users to create AI-generated LinkedIn posts using keyboard input or voice-to-text transcription. While most data is stored on the user’s iPhone, certain data is transmitted to OpenAI for processing to help create the posts.

2. Data Collection and Transmission

When you use "Lek" to create posts, some of the input data (e.g., text or voice transcriptions) is transmitted to OpenAI’s servers to generate personalized content using AI. This data is processed by OpenAI solely for the purpose of generating content based on your input and is not stored by Blank Canvas Designs Private Limited.

3. Data Retention

We do not retain any of your personal data or content after the AI process is completed. All data remains stored on your device after the AI generation is completed, and we do not store any user data on external servers.

4. User Control and Data Deletion

You have full control over the data stored on your device. Deleting the app from your iPhone will permanently delete all associated data, and no information will remain with Blank Canvas Designs Private Limited. Data transmitted to OpenAI is processed in real-time for content generation and is not retained for future use.

5. Third-Party Data Processing (OpenAI)

Data provided to OpenAI is subject to OpenAI’s privacy policy. OpenAI processes your data only to provide the requested AI-generated content. We encourage you to review OpenAI’s privacy policy for more information on their data practices.

OpenAI’s Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or store data from individuals under 13, and if we become aware that such data has been provided, it will be promptly deleted.

7. Security

While we ensure that the app follows best practices for security within the iOS environment, we recommend users take standard precautions to protect their device, such as using strong passwords and enabling device security features.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any significant changes through an update in the app. The latest version of the Privacy Policy will always be available within the app and on our website.
